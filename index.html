Perry Fustero

Full Stack Web Developer

Web developer who is tenacious about finding solutions to problems. I am a triple threat programmer, educator, and musician who views each of my passions as an opportunity for innovation. I am a recent graduate of the Web Development Immersive program from General Assembly in Washington DC. I am looking for work in front-end and full-stack web development.


Some technologies that interest me the most are front-end frameworks like AngularJS and React, web APIs, Websockets, and game development. When I'm not coding I enjoy playing piano, hiking, and reading.

My Projects

Simon Piano

For my first GA project, I chose to create a 'Simon' type of game on a piano keyboard. This game was inspired by an ear training game I would use with my students when I was a music instructor. The students would play back a melody after first hearing me play it. The technologies I used on this project were Javascript, CSS, HTML, and Git for version control.

I fell in love with this project and had a blast creating it. After getting the basic functionality of a 'Simon' game working, I was able to introduce a variety of other features, like binding the piano keys to a computer keyboard, turning the key lights on or off, free play mode, and even a few songs.
